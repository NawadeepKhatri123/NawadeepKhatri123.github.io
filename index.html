About Canada

  • Canada has the longest coastline in the world.
  • It has 10 provinces and 3 territories.
  • Hockey is the most popular sport in Canada.
  • Canada has two official languages: English and French.
  • The maple leaf is the national symbol of Canada.
  • 70% of the maple syrup comes from Canada.
  • The country spans six different time zones.
  • The country has more lakes than the rest of the world.
